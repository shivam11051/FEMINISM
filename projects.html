Empowering Change Through Projects

Femprish undertakes various projects to make a meaningful impact on gender equality in India. Here are some of our ongoing initiatives:

  • Project 1:


    Educate Her - Providing education and skills training to underprivileged girls

  • Project 2:


    Break the Silence - Raising awareness about domestic violence and providing support to survivors

  • Project 3:


    Workplace Equality - Advocating for fair and inclusive workplaces

  • Project 4:


    Community Engagement - Collaborating with local communities to address social issues and promote gender equality
