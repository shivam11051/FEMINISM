Welcome to Femprish

Femprish is a non-profit organization dedicated to working towards equality for all. Our mission is to eliminate the negative connotation that accompanies the term "feminist." We believe in creating a society where everyone, regardless of gender, can thrive and have equal opportunities.

With a particular focus on India, we strive to address the unique challenges faced by women and marginalized communities in the country. Through our various projects and initiatives, we aim to create a more inclusive and equitable society for all.
